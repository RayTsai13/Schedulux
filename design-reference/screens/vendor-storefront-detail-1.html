The Legacy

Founded by Elias Thorne, Elder & Ironwood began as a small workshop in a repurposed cedar mill. Our philosophy is simple: we do not cut down living trees for our work.

Instead, we partner with local arborists to salvage trees lost to storms or disease. This "Second Life" approach ensures that every table, chair, and bowl retains the soul of the Cascades.
